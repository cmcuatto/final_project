Located in Plainville, Connecticut, Cristal Commons provides spacious, affordable apartments, retail spaces and storage garages for rent. There are varing apartment sizes offered in one or two bedroom layouts. Please inquire for availabiliy.

Plainville is a growing suburb of Hartford that encompasses small-town charm with the easy accessibility to two of Connecticut’s largest cities, Hartford and Waterbury. Cristal Commons has been a prominent family-owned business for over 40 years. Owners Joe and Maria Cristofaro have been residents of Plainville for over 50 years and owned the town’s cleaners, Yale Cleaners, before selling it in 1998. The Cristofaros look forward to meeting with all potential residents.
